Floating Hull Simulation

Learning Objectives

  • Understand how buoyant force relates to displaced water volume.
  • Explore how hull shape, width, and depth affect flotation.
  • Observe pressure distribution and resulting floating depth.

Instructions

  1. Select a hull shape from the dropdown.
  2. Adjust the width and depth sliders to change the hull dimensions.
  3. Set the load slider to add mass onto the hull.
  4. Predict whether the hull will float or sink, then observe the result.
  5. Click "Reset" to clear and try new configurations.
